Departure VIP Assistance at Phuket Airport

Departure VIP support helps travelers leave Phuket through HKT with a smoother terminal process, from meeting outside the departure terminal to priority immigration and gate guidance.

Meeting point: departure terminal entrance.
Airport: Phuket International Airport, HKT.
Availability: all international departures, 24/7.
Price: from THB 1,700 per person.

What Departure VIP Includes

  • Personal meeting at the agreed terminal entrance point.
  • Check-in assistance when airline counter support is needed.
  • Priority immigration and passport control guidance.
  • Security lane guidance and terminal navigation.
  • Gate escort and chat support until the service is complete.

Best For

Departure VIP is recommended for tight flight schedules, business travelers, families, elderly passengers, and peak-season departures when check-in and passport-control queues are harder to predict.

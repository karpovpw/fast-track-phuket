VIP Fast Track Phuket Airport (HKT) FAQ

How much time does VIP Fast Track Phuket Airport (HKT) save?

During peak periods, regular immigration at Phuket Airport can take 60 minutes or more. VIP Fast Track usually clears immigration in under 5 minutes.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

The team monitors flights in real time and adjusts the escort schedule automatically when a flight is delayed.

Does Fast Track work for all airlines at HKT?

Yes. The service is available for international flights at Phuket International Airport, including major full-service and low-cost airlines.

Do travelers still need TDAC?

Yes. Travelers entering Thailand must complete the Thailand Digital Arrival Card when required. Fast Track can help with airport processing, but TDAC should be prepared before arrival.

How do I book?

Send your flight details via WhatsApp or Telegram. The team confirms the booking, meeting point, and escort details before the service.

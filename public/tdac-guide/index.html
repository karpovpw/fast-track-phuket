Thailand Digital Arrival Card (TDAC) Guide for Phuket Airport

The Thailand Digital Arrival Card is required for international travelers entering Thailand. If you arrive through Phuket International Airport (HKT), prepare the TDAC before arrival and keep the confirmation QR code available for immigration.

TDAC Checklist for HKT Arrivals

  • Passport details for each traveler.
  • Arrival flight number and arrival date.
  • Accommodation address in Thailand.
  • Contact details and travel information requested by the official form.
  • Saved TDAC confirmation or QR code on your mobile device.

Avoid Unofficial Paid Forms

The official government TDAC portal is free. Do not pay third-party websites that imitate the form or charge unnecessary service fees. If you book the Phuket Airport Fast Track combo package, TDAC guidance is included as part of the support.

How TDAC Relates to Fast Track

Fast Track helps with airport flow and priority immigration processing, but it does not remove the traveler requirement to complete TDAC. Having TDAC ready before landing makes the VIP arrival process smoother.

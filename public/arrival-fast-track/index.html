Arrival Fast Track at Phuket Airport (HKT)

Arrival Fast Track is for international passengers landing at Phuket International Airport who want to avoid long immigration queues and move from aircraft arrival to airport exit with personal assistance.

Book on WhatsApp Book on Telegram
Processing time: usually under 5 minutes at immigration.
Airport: Phuket International Airport, HKT.
Availability: all international arrivals, 24/7.
Price: from THB 1,600 per person.

What Arrival Fast Track Includes

  • Personal greeting at the aircraft bridge or immigration-zone meeting point.
  • Escort to the priority immigration lane at Phuket Airport.
  • Terminal guidance after passport control.
  • Help coordinating luggage, customs flow, taxi, or driver meeting when needed.
  • Real-time flight monitoring, with automatic schedule adjustment for delays.

Who Should Use It

This service is most useful for families with children, business travelers, elderly passengers, late-night arrivals, and anyone arriving during Phuket peak season from November to March, when regular immigration queues can be long.
